About Sachin P. Sudame, Advocate

Established in 2000 and located in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Sudame & Associates is a renowned law firm led by Sachin P. Sudame, a seasoned advocate with a sterling reputation for professionalism and expertise in the legal domain.

Services Offered

  • Consulting services
  • Search, title verification reports, vetting, and due diligence
  • Drafting and conveyancing of deeds and documents (e.g., sale deed, deed of apartment, gift deed)
  • Drafting and registration of mortgages
  • Civil litigation for property disputes
  • Bank recovery proceedings under the Securitisation Act and other relevant statutes
  • Criminal litigations
  • Arbitration and conciliation matters

Office Infrastructure
